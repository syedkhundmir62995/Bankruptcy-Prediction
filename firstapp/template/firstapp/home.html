WHAT IS BANKRUPTCY

Bankruptcy is the inability of a person or a business to pay their debts. It involves the sale of property and some other arrangement to pay as much as possible of the money a person or business entity owe. When a company declares bankruptcy, it’s a sign that it is no longer able to pay the debts as originally agreed and it can seriously damage the company’s credit history for more than 10 years.
